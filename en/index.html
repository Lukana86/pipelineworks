The problem

Why leads fall through the cracks

Ad agencies get you clicks. CRM consultants set up your software. But leads still get lost in the gaps between the two — a form no one follows up on, a page that doesn't convert, a lead passed to sales too early or too late. In B2B, that handoff gap is the single biggest source of lost revenue.

A lead pipeline is only as strong as its weakest handoff. Its job is throughput — moving every lead from acquisition to conversion smoothly, so value isn't lost in any single stage. That's why we own the whole flow, not just one part of it.
