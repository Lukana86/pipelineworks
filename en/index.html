The gap

Most agencies stop at the click. Industrial sales starts after it.

Generic agencies optimise for clicks and form fills. But industrial B2B doesn't buy from a landing page. It buys through long cycles, distributor networks, technical evaluations and multiple decision-makers. That's where most pipelines silently leak: between the campaign and the sales conversation.

Revenue rests on existing customers. New ones aren't coming.

Campaigns speak marketing. Buyers speak technical.

Leads arrive. The handoff fails.

Sales gets cold leads instead of ready ones.
