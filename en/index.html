Why it matters

Why leads fall through the cracks

Ad agencies get you clicks. CRM consultants set up your software. But leads still get lost in the gaps between the two. A form no one follows up on. A page that doesn't convert. A lead passed to sales too early or too late. In B2B, that handoff is the single biggest source of lost revenue.

A lead pipeline is only as strong as its weakest handoff. Its real job is throughput: moving every lead smoothly from acquisition to conversion, so value isn't lost at any single stage. That is why we run the whole flow, not just one part of it.
